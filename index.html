Shirley Marksman

A mother, grandmother, sister, and nurse with a heart of gold.

Here's a timeline of Shirley Marksman's life:

  • 1935 - Born on the island of St. Vincent as the oldest of what would eventually be nine children.
  • 1949 - Arrived in Brooklyn, New York, USA to her grandmother's care.
  • 1955 - Graduated with her Diploma in Nursing as a Registered Nurse (RN).
  • 1957 - Graduated with her Bachelor of Science degree in Nursing.
  • 1965 - Married her first husband on September 4, 1965.
  • 1966 - In September of 1966 she gave birth to their first child: a daughter.
  • 1969 - She arranged for the purchase of a home in Crown Heights, Brooklyn.
  • 1971 - In January of 1971 she gave birth to their second child: another daughter.
  • 1973 - Her mother passed away. She took on the role of matriarch in the family.
  • 1982 - She and her first husband divorced.
  • 1985 - She married her second husband.
  • 1985 - Her oldest daughter got married.
  • 1987 - She welcomed her first grandchild.
  • 1988 - Her second husband passed away.
  • 1998 - Her first husband passed away. She fell ill the same month.
  • 1998 - She retired from a career that spanned over 40 years as a psychiatric nurse.
  • 2001 - She fell ill after burying her favorite aunt. While in the hospital, the events of September 11 occurred, to her shock and dismay.
  • 2002 - On the night of March 2, 2002 she passed away at home.
